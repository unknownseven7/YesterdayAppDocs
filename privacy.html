Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

Reset: Habit Tracker is designed to store your habits and progress locally on your device. The app does not require an account.

What the app stores

  • Habit names, anchor times, streak data, and local completion history
  • Badge progress and unlock status
  • Whether premium has been unlocked on the device

Payments

One-time purchases are handled by Google Play Billing. Reset does not directly receive your card details. Google may process billing information according to its own privacy terms.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Reset can send local habit reminders and badge unlock notifications. These are generated on your device.

Support emails

If you email habitappemailcontact@gmail.com, your email address and the information you choose to send will be used only to respond to your message.

Data sharing

Reset does not currently sell or share your habit data with third parties.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact habitappemailcontact@gmail.com.
