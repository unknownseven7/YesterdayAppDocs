Yesterday

One moment. One sentence. Every day.

Yesterday is a minimalist daily memory app for Android. It asks one question each day: "What's one thing from today worth remembering?" Over time, those brief notes become a quiet record of ordinary life.

Privacy Policy Contact Support

What makes it different

  • It is not a blank-page journal. There is one prompt and one moment to capture.
  • It is local-first. Your memories stay on your device unless you choose to export or share them.
  • It values ordinary life. Mundane memories count just as much as milestone ones.

What Yesterday includes

  • One daily memory entry
  • A readable archive of everything you have written
  • On This Day resurfacing
  • Montage views that gather past memories into one quieter picture
  • Optional one-time premium unlock for deeper rediscovery, search, export, and sharing
