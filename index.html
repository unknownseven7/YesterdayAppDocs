Reset: Habit Tracker

A calm habit tracker for easily distracted adults. Build a few meaningful routines, check in with one tap, and keep going without shame.

Privacy Policy Support Contact Email

What Reset focuses on

  • One habit for free, unlimited habits after a one-time unlock
  • Simple daily reminders and large tap targets
  • Badges, streaks, and a gentle weekly view
  • No harsh failure language

Examples

Walk for 30 minutes, meditate for 10 minutes, read 10 pages, stretch for 5 minutes, journal for 5 minutes, or take vitamins.
